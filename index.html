About Us

An overview of our course, block section, and the professor who guided our work.

About our
Course / Subject

This project was developed for the subject IT0043 – Web Design with Client-Side Scripting, which introduces students to essential front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

The course emphasizes hands-on learning, encouraging students to apply web design principles, improve user experience, and follow web standards.

About Our
Block Section

We are Block TC03, first-year students from the FEU Institute of Technology, enrolled in the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology with a specialization in Cybersecurity. Our section is composed of dedicated individuals with a strong interest in technology and digital innovation.

As aspiring IT professionals, we are focused on building a strong foundation in both technical knowledge and practical skills.

About Our
Course Professor

Our block was nurtured under the mentorship of Dr. Joferson Bombasi, our professor for IT0043.

Dr. Bombasi is a distinguished individual with extensive experience as an IT professional and educator. He served as the Program Director of CCS at FEU Alabang and was formerly a Computer Science professor at the College of Southern Nevada.

With his support, we were able to apply our learning by building individual portfolio websites, allowing us to showcase both our technical skills and creative approaches to web design.
